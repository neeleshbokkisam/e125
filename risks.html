Common Privacy Risks:

  • Data Breaches: Hackers steal sensitive data like passwords and credit card details.
  • Tracking: Companies track online activity to create user profiles for targeted ads.
  • Identity Theft: Criminals use stolen data to impersonate users and commit fraud.

Real-World Example:

In 2019, a major data breach exposed the personal data of over 100 million people. This highlights the need for stronger security measures.
