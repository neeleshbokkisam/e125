What is Data Privacy?

Data privacy refers to the right of individuals to control how their personal information is collected, used, and shared.

Key Concepts of Data Privacy:

  • Data Ownership: The idea that users own their personal data and decide how it is used.
  • Transparency: Companies should clearly communicate their data practices.
  • Consent: Users must give informed consent before their data is collected.

Example:

When signing up for a social media account, users often provide personal details. Companies may share this information with advertisers, raising concerns about data privacy.
