Welcome to Data Privacy 101

In a world where data is constantly collected and shared, it's crucial to know how to safeguard your privacy. This website will guide you through the basics of data privacy and provide actionable steps to protect yourself online.

Understanding Data Privacy

Data privacy refers to the right of individuals to control how their personal information is collected, used, and shared. Companies track data through cookies, apps, and social media platforms, often without users fully realizing it.

Privacy Risks

  • Data Breaches: Hackers accessing sensitive personal information.
  • Tracking: Companies tracking online behavior for targeted ads.
  • Identity Theft: Personal data being stolen and misused.

How to Protect Your Data

  1. Use strong, unique passwords and a password manager.
  2. Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on your accounts.
  3. Avoid suspicious links and phishing attempts.
  4. Use privacy tools like ad blockers and VPNs.
