Tips to Protect Your Data:

  1. Use strong passwords and a password manager.
  2. Enable two-factor authentication (2FA).
  3. Avoid clicking on suspicious links.
  4. Use privacy tools like VPNs and ad blockers.

Recommended Tools:

Consider using tools like LastPass for password management and NordVPN for secure browsing.
